## Connect Phone, SMS, Email, and Live Chat

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-195.png)
</Frame>

Connect channels before customer traffic starts, then test each channel end to end.

### Phone and SMS

Use Phone/SMS setup to manage the business number that receives calls, sends SMS, and identifies outbound calls.

Test by calling the number from an outside phone and sending a short SMS to confirm the thread appears in Desk.

### Email

Connect the support mailbox your team will reply from. After connecting, send a test customer email and reply from Desk.

If email replies fail, check mailbox authorization, sender settings, and whether the channel still shows as connected.

### Live Chat

Create the Live Chat widget, copy the install script, and place it on the website. Then open the website as a visitor and send a test message.

A Live Chat setup is not complete until a website message reaches Desk and an agent can reply.
