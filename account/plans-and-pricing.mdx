title: "Plans and Pricing"

Solvea offers a **Free Plan** by default for all new accounts. \
If you need more credits or advanced features, you can upgrade to a **Pay-As-You-Go Plan** (starting at \$30/month) or choose a **custom Enterprise Plan** for additional capabilities

You can view your current plan and usage in the **bottom-left corner** of the dashboard sidebar.\
Click **Upgrade** to explore available plans.

## Free Plan (Default)

All new accounts start on the Free Plan, which includes:

- 1,000 credits/month
- 50MB Knowledge Base
- 1 Phone Number
- Email
- Livechat
- Shopify Integration
- Google Calendar & Sheets

## Pay-As-You-Go Plan

You can pay to upgrade for additional monthly credits:

- 30,000 credits — \$30/month
- 50,000 credits — \$50/month
- 70,000 credits — \$70/monthEnterprise Plan

## Enterprise Plan

Custom pricing. Contact Sales for a quote.

Includes everything in Pay-As-You-Go, plus:

- Unlimited product features
- Dedicated training
- Customer Success Manager (CSM)
- AI Self-Learning
- Amazon integration
- Rakuten integration
