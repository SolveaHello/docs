Solvea offers a **Free Plan** by default for all new accounts. \
If you need more credits or advanced features, you can upgrade to a **Basic Plan** (\$30/month) or choose a **custom Enterprise Plan** for additional capabilities

You can view your current plan and usage in the **bottom-left corner** of the dashboard sidebar.\
Click **Upgrade** to explore available plans.

## Free Plan (Default)

All new accounts start on the Free Plan, which includes:

- 1,000 credits/month
- 3 agents
- 50MB Knowledge Base
- A 7-day free trial phone number
- Email
- LivechatShopify Integration
- Google Calendar & Sheets

## Basic Plan

You can pay \$30/month to upgrade for more credits or advanced features:

Includes everything in Free Plan, plus:

- 3,000 credits/month
- 10 agents
- 500MB knowledge base

## Enterprise Plan

Custom pricing. Contact Sales for a quote.

Includes everything in Basic Plan, plus:

- Unlimited product features
- Dedicated training
- Customer Success Manager (CSM)
- AI Self-Learning
- Amazon integration
- Rakuten integration
