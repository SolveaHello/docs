## Credit Consumption Rules

- 1-minute phone call: 30 credits
- 1 email reply: 7 credits
- 1 chat reply: 2 credits
- 1 SMS: 7 credits
- Co-pilot task: Credits per task

Credits reset monthly based on your subscription plan.
