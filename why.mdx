## What the Solvea App Does

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-194.png)
</Frame>

The Solvea App brings your business phone, AI receptionist, customer history, and team availability into one mobile workflow.

Solvea App is built for teams that cannot afford to miss customer calls, but also cannot sit at a desk all day. It gives your business a real phone number, lets AI answer when your team is busy, and saves calls and SMS follow-ups into one Inbox.

Use the app when you need to:

- Receive business calls on iPhone without exposing a personal number.
- Let AI answer common questions, collect customer intent, or cover missed calls.
- Call or text customers from the same business number.
- Review recordings, transcripts, and AI summaries after a conversation.
- Keep customer contact history connected to the person, not scattered across devices.
- See teammate availability and hand off calls or follow-ups.

The app is strongest for mobile voice and SMS work. Use PC Web when you need the full multichannel workspace for Email, Live Chat, channel setup, or larger desk operations.

### Plain-English Value Message

**Solvea App helps small teams answer, understand, and follow up with customers from one business number, even when no one is sitting at a desk.**

### Who Should Use It

- Owners and operators who answer calls while moving around.
- Sales or service teams that need fast customer follow-up.
- Small teams that want AI to cover overflow or after-hours calls.
- Mobile agents who need caller history before they call back.

### What Changes After Setup

Before Solvea, a missed call is easy to lose. After Solvea, the same moment becomes a record: who called, what happened, what the AI or teammate said, and what to do next.
