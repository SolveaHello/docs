<Update label="April 24, 2026" tags={["Improvements"]}>
  This release focuses on two areas: smarter guidance for Vibecoding and expanded voice language support — helping users test faster, configure easier, and reach further.

  ## Vibecoding Upgrades

  ### Smart test guidance

  <Frame>
    <video src="/videos/testcard-small.mp4" controls />
  </Frame>

  When Solvea detects testing intent in Vibecoding, a prompt card appears — choose Voice or Text and start testing instantly, no hunting for the entry point.

  ### Conversational voice setup

  <Frame>
  </Frame>

  In Vibecoding Train, you can now update your Agent's voice model through conversation. Just tell Solvea the language you want — it'll confirm and configure it for you.

  ### Clear language options

  When language configuration comes up in conversation, Solvea will proactively list all currently supported languages so you can choose with confidence.

  ## 7 new languages added

  <Frame>
    ![Image (14)](/images/image-\(14\).png)
  </Frame>

  Solvea now supports French, German, Indonesian, Italian, Portuguese, Romanian, and Thai — your Agent can speak to even more users around the world.
</Update>

  ## Voice Quality Improvements

  <Frame>
    ![Image](/images/image-192.png)
  </Frame>

  We've resolved a set of persistent audio issues affecting call clarity.

  Swallowed words are fixed, and problems caused by microphone jitter, roughness, or unclear input on the caller's end are now detected and corrected automatically.

  <Frame>
    ![Image](/images/image-193.png)
  </Frame>

  Solvea significantly improved how the AI searches and applies your knowledge base, reducing off-topic or inaccurate responses and making every answer more reliable.
</Update>

<Update label="March 27, 2026" tags={["New releases"]}>
  ## Shopify Integration — Your AI Agent, Built for E-Commerce

  <video src="/videos/shopify-guide-small.mp4" controls />

  Solvea now connects directly to Shopify. Link your store once, and your agent instantly has access to your full product catalog and order data — ready to recommend, answer questions, and resolve issues.

  With Shopify connected, your agent can:

  - Recommend the right products based on real-time product knowledge.
  - Look up order status, shipping details on demand
  - Run as a Livechat widget directly on your store for live support

  [User Guide](/deploy/e-commerce-platform/shopify)
</Update>
