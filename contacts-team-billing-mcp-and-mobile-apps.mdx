Contacts, team, billing, mobile app links, and MCP access support the daily Desk workflow.

### Contacts

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-195.png)
</Frame>

Contacts help the team recognize customers across calls, SMS, email, and tickets. Open a contact to review details and history before calling or replying.

### Team Members

Invite teammates, manage seats, and check availability. Keep the number of paid seats aligned with the people who actually work in Desk or answer calls.

### Billing and Usage

Billing shows subscription state, seats, numbers, and credit usage. Usage helps you understand where costs come from: calls, SMS, AI processing, translation, number rental, or outbound work.

Do not hardcode prices in help content. Tell users to rely on what the Billing page shows.

### Mobile Apps

Use **Settings \> Mobile Apps** to help teammates install the app for mobile calling and SMS follow-up.

### MCP Access

MCP keys are for external integrations and trusted tools. Generate a key only when needed, keep it private, and revoke it when the connected tool is no longer used.
